Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Which AI video tool is truly free?

CapCut offers the most generous free tier — AI effects, auto-editing, and auto-captions are all included with minimal restrictions. Pika and Runway also have free plans, but with limits on generation count and resolution.

Q. What's the best tool for realistic text-to-video?

Runway Gen-4 is currently the leader in quality, producing cinematic-grade footage from text prompts. It's used by professional filmmakers and studios. Pika is a strong second choice, especially for social media short-form content.

Q. I want AI avatars that speak — which tool?

HeyGen and Synthesia are the two market leaders. HeyGen excels at natural expressions and instant multilingual dubbing. Synthesia is built for enterprise with strong security and compliance features. Choose based on your use case.

Q. Can I create AI videos on my phone?

CapCut has the most polished mobile app experience, with seamless TikTok integration. You can shoot, AI-edit, auto-caption, and publish all from your phone — a complete one-stop workflow.
