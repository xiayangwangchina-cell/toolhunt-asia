FAQ

Q. What's the best free AI voice tool?

ElevenLabs (10,000 chars/month free), VoiceMaker (250 chars per use), and Aitubo (generous free tier) all offer free plans. ElevenLabs stands out for its exceptional quality even on the free plan — definitely worth trying first.

Q. Which tool has the best Japanese & Korean voice quality?

ElevenLabs leads by a wide margin for both Japanese and Korean speech synthesis, with near-native intonation and emotional expressiveness. Aitubo is a strong runner-up with its dedicated Asian language optimization.

Q. Can I use AI voices commercially (YouTube, ads, etc.)?

Yes — all five tools allow commercial use. However, free tiers may require attribution. ElevenLabs paid plans (starting at $5/month) offer full commercial rights with no attribution needed, making it the most creator-friendly option.

Q. Can I clone my own voice?

ElevenLabs offers professional voice cloning on paid plans — just upload a short voice sample and the AI recreates a near-identical digital voice. Murf and PlayHT have similar features, but ElevenLabs is widely regarded as the most accurate.
